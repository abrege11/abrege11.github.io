Explore the notation and function of the hadamard, Pauli-X, Pauli-Y, Pauli-Z, Swap, Phase, CNOT, controlled Z, T, and Toffoli gates

For each gate, we will begin by processing both a \( \ket{0} \) and \( \ket{1} \). Then, we will proceed to process different values to ensure you understand what is going on.

Pauli-X: \[ X = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \] \( \ket{0} \rightarrow \ket{1} \) and \( \ket{1} \rightarrow \ket{0} \)

Pauli-Y: \[ X = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \] \( \ket{0} \rightarrow i\ket{1} \) and \( \ket{1} \rightarrow -i\ket{0} \)

Pauli-Y: \[ X = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & -i \\ i & 0 \\ \end{pmatrix} \] \( \ket{0} \rightarrow i\ket{1} \) and \( \ket{1} \rightarrow -i\ket{0} \)
